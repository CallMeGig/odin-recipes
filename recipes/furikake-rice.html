Furikake Rice

Although it is only made of rice, scrambled eggs, chicken, and green onions, this dish relies on a secret ingredient to make it special: gelatin cubes.

This recipe is a tad difficult if you have little cooking experience, but it gets easier after you've prepared the chicken broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 7-8 chicken wings
  • 2 tsp of bonito or fish soup stock
  • 5-6 cups of water for the broth, and ½ cup of water for the gelatin
  • 2 tsp of grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 3 tbsp of light soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of sake
  • 2 tbsp of gelatin
  • 2 green onions
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of white rice

Serves 1-2

Directions

Broth and chicken wings

  1. Heat the pan and add in sesame oil. Once heated, fry the chicken wings till it’s golden brown on both sides. Keep the sesame oil and set the chicken wings aside.
  2. Pour water and soup stock into a pot till the mixture boils. Add the cooked chicken wings and cook it on medium low heat.
  3. Add ginger, soy sauce, sugar and sake into the sesame oil in a pan. Mix well.
  4. Transfer everything into the simmering broth for 20 minutes. Remove the layer of scum, if any.
  5. Remove the chicken wings from the pot and set aside.

Gelatin

  1. Mix gelatin and half a cup of water together, then add into the broth. Mix well.
  2. Prepare a tray and coat it with butter. Add broth and wrap it with cling film before putting it into the fridge for 2 hours.
  3. The end product is a solidified broth. Slice into small cubes.

Scrambled Eggs

  1. Add eggs into a bowl and whisk them.
  2. Prepare a pan on low heat and pour in the whisked eggs.
  3. Continue whisking the eggs on the heated pan until it is semi-solidified.

Furikake Rice

  1. Slice green onions into fine bits.
  2. Prepare a bowl of rice and add scrambled eggs until it covers the rice.
  3. Debone the chicken wings and add it onto your rice, or place it separately on another plate.
  4. Sprinkle the gelatin cubes and watch it melt over the eggs and rice.
